# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 20, 2026**

Egg is a personal AI agent built and operated by a single individual for personal use. This policy describes how the Oura integration accesses and uses your data.

## What data is accessed

When the Oura integration is enabled, Egg accesses your Oura Ring sleep data via the Oura API, specifically:

- Sleep session start and end times
- Sleep score and readiness data (to determine wake-up time)

## How data is used

Oura data is used solely to detect when you wake up and trigger a personalized good morning message sent to you via iMessage on your own device. No data is shared with third parties.

## Data storage

Oura API responses are processed in memory and not persisted to disk. No Oura data is written to files, databases, or transmitted beyond your local machine.

## Who has access

This is a single-user personal application. Only you (the operator) have access to your data. No other parties access or receive your Oura data.

## Third-party services

Egg uses the [Oura API](https://cloud.ouraring.com/docs) to retrieve sleep data. Your use of the Oura ring and API is subject to [Oura's own Privacy Policy](https://ouraring.com/privacy-policy).

## Contact

This is a personal open-source project. For questions, open an issue at the project repository.
